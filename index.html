Program Overview

Clear selection → hands-on collaboration → performance-based stipend.

◈ Selection Process

  • Round 1: Aptitude — logic & analytical thinking
  • Round 2: Coding — problem-solving & code quality
  • Round 3: Interview — mindset, collaboration, ownership

  • Collaborate on live projects with peers across India
  • Receive stipend or project-based payment based on contribution

Terms, Legality & Contact

✔ Legal Notice

  • Collabs99 is a student-led collaboration program, not a company recruitment drive.
  • Any registration/payment details are shared only inside the official WhatsApp group. Do not pay outside the specified instructions.
  • Selection is based on aptitude, coding, and interview. Only selected students collaborate on projects.
  • This is not an employment offer and does not guarantee long-term employment.
  • Selected collaborators may receive a stipend or project-based payment depending on contribution and project success.

✳ Code of Conduct

  • Respect, honesty, and zero tolerance for plagiarism.
  • Commit to timelines; communicate blockers early.
  • Use Git and follow review guidelines.
  • No misrepresentation of association; always state “student collaboration”.
